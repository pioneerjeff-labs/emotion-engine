# Brand Notes

## Lab Brand

Use **PioneerJeff Labs** publicly. Avoid short lab acronyms in public-facing copy; the full name is more distinctive and easier to turn into a memorable mark.

Recommended positioning:

```text
PioneerJeff Labs builds reusable infrastructure layers for creative AI applications.
```

Brand architecture:

- **PioneerJeff Labs** is the lab umbrella for reusable AI infrastructure.
- **Emotion Engine** is an independent product/project name.
- Future projects should also have independent names and turn concrete creative needs into reusable lower-level layers.
- Use **Jeff** or **PioneerJeff** as the founder/persona layer, not as a prefix forced into every project.

Logo direction:

- Keep organization-level avatar and wordmark assets outside this project repository.
- Use a spark or creative-infrastructure mark for the lab, but do not reuse the Emotion Engine mark as the organization avatar.
- Do not design around initials.

## Emotion Engine Brand

Emotion Engine should have its own project mark. A logo makes the repository feel more deliberate and helps future demos, release posts, docs, and videos look coherent.

Recommended direction:

- Avoid hearts, smiley faces, brains, and medical-looking imagery.
- Use signals, continuity curves, state nodes, or memory paths.
- Keep the look technical, warm, and inspectable.
